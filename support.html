FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I set up my new NextGen device?

Setting up your NextGen device is simple. Download our NextGen app, create an account, and follow the on-screen instructions. Most devices are ready to use in under 5 minutes.

What is your warranty policy?

All NextGen products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available at purchase.

How do I return a product?

You can return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Visit our returns portal or contact support for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Check our shipping page for details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.
